Gone Fishin'

The WaSP's mailing lists and signup forms are as dead as Boo.com, thanks to a debacle we laughingly refer to as "The Troubles" of Summer 2000. We're working on rebuilding the whole thing from scratch. Bear with us please, and ignore this page for now.

Without you, we're nothing. Help us fight the good fight. Join the few, the proud, The Web Standards Project.
       Fill in the form to become a supporting member. If you hate forms, use the WSP-EZ Short Form on our cover page, or click below to join one of our mailing lists and enroll at the same time.

The WaSP is in the mail.
Subscribe to our standards discussion list and you'll be added to our discussion list. THIS IS A HIGH VOLUME LIST, where members brainstorm ways to spread the WaSP's message effectively.
        If you don't have time for our discussion list, subscribe to the WSP Announce list. You'll get important announcements – and nothing else.
