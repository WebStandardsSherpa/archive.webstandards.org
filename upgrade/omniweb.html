WHAT ABOUT OMNIWEB?

The makers of OmniWeb are confusing their users by claiming advanced standards compliance for their product on bulletin boards and newsgroups, while their own release notes admit "CSS level 1 and CSS element positioning support is not yet complete" (let alone DOM compliance). See Cocoa Browser Missing Important Features for details.

The WaSP thinks alternative browsers are a fine idea, but companies should be careful not to claim more than they currently deliver. We hope and trust that the makers of OmniWeb are sincere in their dedication, and that the browser they offer will soon offer true standards compliance.
